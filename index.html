Welcome to my homepage!

Yuxing Wang is a third-year master student at Tsinghua University with broad interests in Robotics, Evolutionary Computation, Reinforcement Learning and Embodied AI. Before coming to Tsinghua, he obtained the bachelor’s degree from Southwest Minzu University, China. He had also worked at Tencent AI Lab as a research intern.
